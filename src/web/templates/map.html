Preliminary MT solutions, marked with [A], are automatically derived by the Gisola software. These solutions have not been revised by a seismologist and could contain information of uncertain quality, as opposed to the Reviewed ones, where they are marked with [M].

Solution's quality could take the value A,B,C,D mixed with 1,2,3,4 where A1 is characterized as exceptional case, and D4 denotes unreliable solution.

The geographical position of the MT solutions is set according to the value of the captured hypocenter's location.

The software does not provide any guarantee for this information and the risk derived from improper use is assumed by the user.
